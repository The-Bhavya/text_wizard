Our Algorithms

1

LexRank

A graph-based summarization algorithm that uses the concept of eigenvector centrality in a graph of sentences to determine importance and produce summaries.

2

TextRank

Inspired by Google's PageRank, TextRank is an unsupervised algorithm for text summarization and keyword extraction, utilizing graph-based ranking algorithms.

3

Luhn's Algorithm

One of the oldest text summarization techniques, Luhn's algorithm uses term frequency to identify significant sentences for summarization.

4

Latent Semantic Analysis (LSA)

LSA is a technique that analyzes relationships between a set of documents and the terms they contain by producing a set of concepts related to the documents and terms.
